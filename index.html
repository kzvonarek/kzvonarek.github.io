About Me

Hello! My name is Kristijan. I am a Senior at Newington High School currently pursuing the field of Computer Science. I am very passionate about contributing back to my community and helping teach others about computer science. In addition to computer science I have a large interest in sports and working out. My hobbies include tennis, video games, both creating and playing, and going to the gym.

This year I have just completed a pre-college video game academy at CMU. Within this program I was able to not only learn how to build several projects including a recreation of Q*bert, I was also given strong foundational skills on how to approach production, team management, programming problems, and the application of audio. The program taught me game design methods and ideals, how to lead a team, programming in Unity using C++, audio design through Reaper, and art using photoshop. I was able to work with a variety of group members throughout the 6 weeks. From this program I have gained the ability to collaborate effectively within a team setting and become a responsible adult.
